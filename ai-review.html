Bottom line

The infrastructure buildout is accelerating at unprecedented scale — $91B from Alphabet alone. But enterprise adoption remains stuck at 5%, creating a massive gap between investment and realized value. The real alpha in 2026 will be in applications, not infrastructure. OpenAI and Anthropic are both "too big to fail" but pursuing different strategies. The next 1-2 years could see AI capabilities that reshape everything.

Invest in applications. Watch the adoption curve. Don't confuse capex with value creation.
