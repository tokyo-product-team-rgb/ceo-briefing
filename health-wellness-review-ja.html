注目ストーリー

Wellness market hits record $6.8 trillion. US spending alone reached $2T for the first time. Gen Z and millennials driving growth — 30% focusing "a lot more" on wellness than last year. Consumer skepticism rising: 82% want clearer labels, 71% willing to pay premium for transparent, ethically sourced products. Mental wellness (+10.1% CAGR) and wellness real estate (+15.8%) fastest-growing segments.

主要動向

AI Wearables

AI-powered wearables with real-time adaptive coaching proliferate

Smart rings, mirrors, patches, and earbuds now feature LLM-powered coaching (e.g., Whoop Coach). Devices provide personalized real-time guidance based on biometric data. CES 2026 showcased the next generation of health wearables.

GLP-1

Eli Lilly's retatrutide Phase 3 confirms 25% weight loss

Triple-agonist compound continues outperforming Mounjaro in trials. FDA filing on track for H2 2026. Analysts now project $50B+ peak sales for GLP-1 class by 2030. Food and fitness industries adapting strategies.

AI Drug Discovery

30% of new drugs now discovered via generative AI

AI accelerating drug discovery timelines by 50%+. Early disease detection and personalized treatments becoming standard. Pharma R&D budgets shifting toward AI infrastructure.

Mental Health

Mental wellness segment grows 10.1% annually — fastest in sector

Post-pandemic work-life shifts driving sustained demand. AI-powered mental health tools gaining traction alongside traditional therapy. Youth anxiety remains elevated, spurring innovation.

Wellness Real Estate

Wellness real estate grows 15.8% annually — healthier living spaces boom

Demand for air filtration, circadian lighting, and biophilic design surging. Premium developments command 10-25% price premiums. Japan aging population driving accessible design innovation.

Social Wellness

Social bathhouses and communal therapy clubs expand globally

Contrast therapy clubs (e.g., Arc in London) tapping social wellness trend. Combines cold plunge, sauna, and community. Thermal/mineral springs growing 10% annually for health conditions.

まとめ

The wellness market's $6.8T scale confirms health is now a lifestyle category, not just healthcare. AI is transforming both consumer wearables (real-time coaching) and pharmaceutical R&D (30% of new drugs). GLP-1s continue reshaping food, fitness, and pharma simultaneously. Consumer demand for transparency and ethical sourcing is rising — 71% willing to pay more. Mental wellness and wellness real estate are the fastest-growing segments.

Wellness mainstream. AI enabling personalization. GLP-1s transformative. Transparency demanded.
