The big story

AI dominates as mega-rounds surge 77%. 2025 delivered $469B in global VC — the highest since 2022 — with 65% concentrated in just 738 mega-rounds. AI captured nearly half of all funding ($226B), and the six largest rounds totaled over $111B combined. The bifurcation is now structural: record capital for AI infrastructure, drought for everything else. Project Stargate's $500B US infrastructure commitment signals government-scale AI ambitions.

Top industry insights

Market shift

"65% of VC is now mega-rounds — the barbell is real"

Sequoia Capital research

738 mega-rounds captured $307B in 2025, up 77% from prior year. Meanwhile, deal volume dropped 17% to 29,501 deals. "Capital is concentrating at the top at an unprecedented rate."

AI dominance

"AI is now half of all venture capital"

PitchBook Q4 2025 analysis

AI startups captured $226B — 48% of total VC. The six largest rounds (all AI) totaled $111B+. "There are two venture markets now: AI and everything else."

Fintech

"Fintech's comeback driven by later-stage concentration"

CB Insights fintech report

Fintech hit $51.8B (up 27% YoY) but across 23% fewer deals. Ramp ($32B), Rippling, and prediction markets like Kalshi ($11B) dominated. "Early-stage fintech remains challenging; growth-stage is thriving."

Crypto

"Prediction markets and exchanges lead crypto revival"

Paradigm

Polymarket ($2B from NYSE parent ICE), Kalshi ($1B Series E at $11B), Kraken ($800M at $20B). Regulatory clarity and real-world use cases driving institutional capital back.

Japan

"Japan VC up 40% as Takaichi policies boost startup ecosystem"

JVCA quarterly report

Government incentives and deregulation driving record inbound LP interest. PayPay IPO pipeline, Sakana AI unicorn status, and SoftBank's OpenAI gains validating the Japan thesis.

Warning

"Seed-to-A conversion at historic lows outside AI"

Index Ventures

Non-AI startups need 3x the metrics for half the valuation. "The Series A crunch is structural, not cyclical."
