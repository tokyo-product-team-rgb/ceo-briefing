The big story

AI's insatiable energy demand drives nuclear renaissance. Microsoft, Google, and Amazon signing PPAs with nuclear operators. AI datacenters now account for 58% of new nuclear power agreements. Meanwhile, Japan under Takaichi accelerating nuclear restarts to power industrial competitiveness.

Key developments

Nuclear

Microsoft signs 20-year nuclear PPA to power AI datacenters

Constellation Energy deal provides 835 MW of carbon-free power for Azure AI infrastructure. Largest corporate nuclear agreement ever. Sets template for hyperscaler energy strategy.

Japan

Takaichi government accelerates nuclear restart program

7 additional reactors approved for restart in 2026. Nuclear to provide 22% of Japan's electricity by 2030. Industrial competitiveness and energy security cited.

Fusion

Commonwealth Fusion achieves sustained plasma for 8 seconds

SPARC tokamak demonstrating commercial viability path. $1.8B Series D imminent at $8B+ valuation. Timeline to grid power: late 2030s.

Solar

Global solar capacity crosses 2 terawatts

China accounts for 60% of installations. Cost now below $20/MWh unsubsidized in optimal locations. Storage remains the bottleneck.

Battery

CATL solid-state battery enters mass production

1,000 km range EVs now possible. Energy density 500 Wh/kg — double current lithium-ion. Toyota and BYD racing to catch up.

Bottom line

AI's energy appetite is reshaping the power sector. Nuclear is having a moment — hyperscalers need 24/7 carbon-free power that solar alone can't provide. Japan is betting on nuclear restarts for industrial competitiveness. Fusion continues advancing but remains a 2030s story. The energy transition is accelerating, but reliability is now the constraint.
