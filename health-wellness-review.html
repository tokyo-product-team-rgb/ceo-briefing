Mounjaro targets two hormones (GLP-1 + GIP) vs Ozempic's one. Real-world data suggests Mounjaro leads to greater weight loss for many patients. Eli Lilly launched Mounjaro in India (Aug 2025).

FDA Approvals & Breakthroughs

Opioid-Free

Suzetrigine (Journavax): First Opioid-Free Painkiller

First NaV1.8 blocker to win FDA approval (early 2025). Trials showed opioid-level pain relief for acute and post-surgical pain but without respiratory depression, sedation, or addiction risk.

Fast Track

IHL-42X for Sleep Apnea Gets FDA Fast Track

IXHL's drug candidate IHL-42X received Fast Track designation (Dec 2025) for obstructive sleep apnea treatment, expediting development and approval process.

Sepsis

Breakthrough Sepsis Drug Shows Promise in Phase II

Australian researchers' carbohydrate-based treatment showed promising results in 180-patient Phase II trial, calming dangerous immune reactions in sepsis patients.

Cancer Vaccine

Transgene TG4050: Personalized Cancer Vaccine

French biotech Transgene released Phase I data showing "striking results" for individualized cancer vaccine TG4050 in high-risk head and neck cancer patients.
